SAMPLE ARTICLE 2: Nature
=========================
Title: Rainforest Biodiversity Under Threat
Category: Nature
Image URL: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1441974231531-c6227db76b6e?w=800
Short Description: Scientists warn that deforestation and climate change are rapidly destroying Earth's most biodiverse ecosystems, the rainforests.
Full Article:
The world's rainforests are home to approximately 80% of all animal and plant species on Earth. These vital ecosystems cover only about 6% of the planet's surface but contain incredible biodiversity.

Rainforests play a crucial role in maintaining our planet's climate. They absorb massive amounts of carbon dioxide and produce significant portions of the world's oxygen supply. The loss of rainforests directly impacts global climate patterns.

Recent studies show alarming deforestation rates:

- The Amazon rainforest loses approximately 137 species per day due to rainforest destruction
- Over 70% of Africa's remaining rainforests could be lost by 2050
- Every minute, rainforest the size of 40 football fields is destroyed

Causes of rainforest destruction include:
1. Agricultural expansion (cattle ranching, soy production)
2. Logging for timber and paper products
3. Mining operations
4. Infrastructure development
5. Climate change and wildfires

Conservation efforts are underway through national parks, wildlife reserves, and international agreements. Protecting rainforests is critical for biodiversity and climate stability.

Recent Development:
Leading technology companies have demonstrated quantum computers performing calculations that would take classical computers thousands of years to complete. This milestone, known as quantum advantage, marks a turning point in computing evolution.

Key Advantages of Quantum Computing:
- Exponential speed for specific problem types
- Optimization of complex systems
- Drug discovery and molecular simulation
- Cryptography and security applications

- Maintaining quantum coherence (qubits are extremely fragile)

1. Drug discovery for diseases
2. Materials science advancements
3. Climate modeling and solutions
4. Battery and solar technology improvements
5. Financial optimization

SAMPLE ARTICLE 5: Nature
==========================
Title: Ocean Acidification and Marine Life
Category: Nature
Image URL: https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1559827260-dc66d52bef19?w=800
Short Description: Rising ocean acidity levels threaten marine ecosystems and the food chains that depend on them, with cascading effects throughout the planet.
Full Article:
The world's oceans are becoming increasingly acidic due to absorption of carbon dioxide from the atmosphere. This process, called ocean acidification, is having profound effects on marine life.

The Science:
When CO2 dissolves in seawater, it forms carbonic acid, lowering the pH of the ocean. Ocean pH has decreased by 0.1 units since the Industrial Revolution, representing a 30% increase in acidity.

Affected Marine Species:
- Pteropods (sea butterflies) have difficulty forming shells
- Coral bleaching and reduced calcification
- Fish larvae development issues
- Shellfish hatchery failures
- Algae and plankton affected (base of food chain)

Ecological Consequences:
The disruption of marine food chains affects species at every level. Fish that depend on plankton face food scarcity, which impacts larger predators and eventually commercial fishing industries.

Economic Impact:
Billions of people depend on seafood for protein. Ocean acidification threatens:
- Commercial fishing industries
- Aquaculture operations
- Tourism and recreation
- Food security in developing nations

Solutions Being Pursued:
1. Reducing global carbon emissions
2. Ocean restoration projects
3. Sustainable aquaculture practices
4. Protected marine areas
5. Alternative protein sources

üêõ COMMON ISSUES & FIXES
------------------------

Issue: Articles not showing
Fix: 1. Check Firebase is initialized
     2. Verify firebaseConfig.js has correct credentials
     3. Check Firestore has posts collection
     4. Check security rules allow read access

Issue: Images not displaying
Fix: 1. Verify image URL is correct (test in browser)
     2. Ensure URL is HTTPS (some CDNs block HTTP)
     3. Check CORS headers (some sites block external requests)

Issue: Login not working
Fix: 1. Verify admin user exists in Firebase Auth
     2. Check email and password are correct
     3. Clear browser cache
     4. Try in incognito mode

Issue: Changes not updating on homepage
Fix: 1. Refresh browser page
     2. Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
     3. Check browser console for errors
     4. Verify Firestore connection
